Assignment Seven:
Painting

Even though our software is called Photoshop, don't let that fool you. It can create a wide range of artistic looking pieces. Using a combination of tools you can make a photographic image look like a painting.

In this module, we will go over how to make your photos look like paintings. View the painting demo video and complete the project, which is due Saturday, October 13, 2018 at 11:59pm EST.
