Week Eleven:
Video Clips

Sometimes you may need to quickly edit a video or crop it to a specific length. While there are many video editing programs out there, sometimes it just takes to long to learn how to use them or to properly set them up.

And while Photoshop's name would imply that photos are the only thing that it can work with, this module will show you that in reality it can do a lot more.

In this module we will go over how to create a short video clip/effect. View the video clip demo and complete the assignment, which is due Saturday, April 1, 2017 at 11:59pm EST.
