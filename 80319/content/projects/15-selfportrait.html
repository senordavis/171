Project Description

For this project you will work on a portrait of yourself. But instead of it being just a simple picture of yourself, you will use all of the different skills that you have learned throughout the semester to create a movie poster starring yourself.

The final image for this project will be a composite image that, just like a movie poster, tells a story about you and the crazy world that you inhabit. Source imagery and content is completely up to you, but the final image needs to be more than a photo of you with some filters applied.

Remember that you want to respect both the original images and the pixels used in the digital file. Your intention is not necessarily to create a simple collage of elements, but rather to create an artistic statement. Remember to take into consideration things like composition, focal point, color, texture, technique, etc.

Use the digital tools that Photoshop offers to add the different effects needed using the techniques we have already learned to give yourself the most flexibility when building your design.

Project Specifications

To satisfy the project requirements you will need to submit one finished high-resolution Photoshop image at least 1944 pixels by 2880 pixels. You will also need to submit your original un-retouched images along with your final image so that I can see where you started. You can include the original images as separate layers in the final image file.

Save your image with appropriate names, following the file naming convention:


  yourname-self-portrait.psd

Once completed, upload your files to Self Portrait Dropbox.

Please make sure to upload your files as PSDs. If your file is to large, please upload your PSD to the LCC Google Drive and share a link to the file with me. Then upload a JPG to the D2L Dropbox for this assignment.

If you are having problems please view the Upload guide or email me.
