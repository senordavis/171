Objectives

After successful completion of this assignment, the student will be:

  • Familiarized with the Photshop Interface.

Assignment Specifications

Create anything you would like to, using Photoshop. It can be a photo composite, a collage of images, a drawing, or a painting.

This assignment is to get you started working in Photoshop and getting you familiarized with its interface and tools.

Save your file with the appropriate name, following the file naming convention: 


  yourname-intro.psd

Once completed and named, upload your file to the Photoshop Intro Assignment Submission Folder.

notification_important

Please make sure to upload your files as PSDs. If it exceeds the file size allowed by D2L, please upload the PSD to the LCC Google Drive and share a link to it with me. Then upload a JPG to the D2L Assignment Submission Folder for this assignment.

If you are having problems, please view the upload guide video, or email me.

Grading Criteria

For this project the grading criteria is broken down into four parts.

Category 3 2 1 0
Image Quality

3 points

All images are high quality and meet the requirments.

2 points

Most images are high quality and meet the requirments.

1 point

A few images are high quality and meet the requirments, but many are low quality or do not meet the requirments.

0 points

Images are low quality or do not meet the requirments.
Precision and Creativity

3 points
