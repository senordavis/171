Assignment Five:
Color Correction

Often times you will have photos that you will want to use but the color is way wrong. An important skill to learn is how to correct the color of photos to make them usable in your final designs.

In this module we will go over some of the tools inside of Photoshop to make these corrections so that our photos look the best they can. View the color correction demo and complete the assignment, which is due Saturday, June 23, 2018 at 11:59pm EST.

Assignment Six:
Photo Restoration

As well as correcting the color of your images you may find that you will need to restore an old photograph. While there are many professionals and retail outlets that offer this service, this module will cover how you can take an old photo and restore it yourself.

In this module we will go over how to restore damaged photos. View the photo restoration demo and complete the assignment, which is due Saturday, June 23, 2018 at 11:59pm EST.
