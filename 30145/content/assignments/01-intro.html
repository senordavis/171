Objectives

After successful completion of this assignment, the student will be:

  • Familiarized with the Photshop Interface.

Assignment Specifications

Create anything that you would like to using Photoshop. It can be a photo composit, it can be a collage, it can be a drawing or painting. This assignment is just to get you started working in Photoshop and getting you familiarized with its interface and tools.

Save your file with the appropriate name, following the file naming convention: 


  yourname-intro.psd

Once completed and named, upload your images to the Photoshop Intro Assignemnt Folder .

Please make sure to upload your files as PSDs. If your file is to large, please upload your PSD to the LCC Google Drive and share a link to the file with me. Then upload a JPG to the D2L Dropbox for this assignment.

If you are having problems please view the Upload guide or email me.
