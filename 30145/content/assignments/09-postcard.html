Project Description

The final image will be a composite of image that evoke the style of a vintage postcard. You may chose either a real or imaginary location. To do this project, you will need to collect multiple iconic images of the area. You will need to use your previous knowledge of layers and clipping masks to make the full composition.

Your intention is not necessarily to create a simple collage of images, but rather to create a composition that evokes a sense of place. To that end, be aware of things like layout, composition, focal point, color, texture, and typography.

Project Specifications

To satisfy the project requirements you will need to submit one finished high-resolution Photoshop image that is minimally 5 inches x 7 inches, and has at least 4 iconic images of the location you have chosen.

Save your image with appropriate names, following the file naming convention:


  yourname-travel-postcard.psd


Once completed, upload your images to the Travel Postcard Dropbox.

Please make sure to upload your files as PSDs. If your file is to large, please upload your PSD to the LCC Google Drive and share a link to the file with me. Then upload a JPG to the D2L Dropbox for this assignment.

If you are having problems please view the Upload guide or email me.
