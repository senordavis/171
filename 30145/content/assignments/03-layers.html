Objectives

After successful completion of this assignment, the student will be able to:

  • Place separate images into separate layers.
  • Demonstrate understanding of layer order, styles and blend modes.
  • Execute a believable composited image

Assignment Specifications

In this assignment, you will composite multiple images to create a new image. This picture should feature an image of yourself placed into an unlikely, unique or impossible location.

There should be at least five objects on five layers, including the picture of yourself, and you should try to make it look as “realistic” as possible, given the imaginary nature of the picture.

You might place yourself at the North Pole, for example, climbing the Eiffel Tower, jumping over the moon … or simply standing beside the President at his Inaugural.

At least one layer should contain some text with something like your name, or the location that you are at in your composite image.

Use blend modes and layer effects to enhance the believability of your image.

Save your image scans with appropriate names, following the file naming convention:


  yourname-layers.psd

Once completed, upload your images to the Layers Exercise Dropbox.

Please make sure to upload your files as PSDs. If your file is to large, please upload your PSD to the LCC Google Drive and share a link to the file with me. Then upload a JPG to the D2L Dropbox for this assignment.

If you are having problems please view the Upload guide or email me.
