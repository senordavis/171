Objectives

After successful completion of this assignment, the student will be able to:

  • Place separate images into separate layers.
  • Demonstrate understanding of layer order, styles and blend modes.
  • Execute a believable composited image

Assignment Specifications

Create a collage portrait of yourself of someone else in the spirit of the Italian painter Arcimboldo, like the image seen to the right.

Create a collage portrait of yourself or someone else in the spirit of the Italian painter Arcimboldo, like the image seen below.

To begin, find and download several images of a variety of fruits and vegetables.

Using the selection tools covered in the selections module, select the objects from their backgrounds to manipulate in Photoshop. Create a simple background for your portrait. Use one of your items at least 5 times.

Build your portrait in a new file that is:
5x7 inches at 300 dpi.

Save your image scans with appropriate names, following the file naming convention:


  yourname-selections-portrait.psd

Once completed, upload your images to the Selections Exercise Dropbox.

Please make sure to upload your files as PSDs. If your file is to large, please upload your PSD to the LCC Google Drive and share a link to the file with me. Then upload a JPG to the D2L Dropbox for this assignment.

If you are having problems please view the Upload guide or email me.
