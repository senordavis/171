Week Nine:
Actions

Occasionally while working in Photoshop you may find yourself repeating the same task over and over. Using actions will help automate some of the tasks you repeat most often.

You can very easily record the steps that you take to complete the task and even make modal control stop to control what is happening at key points.

In this module we will go over how to create actions to automate your workflow. View the actions demo video and complete the assignment, which is due Saturday, March 19, 2016 at 11:59pm EST.
