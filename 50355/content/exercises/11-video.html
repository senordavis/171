Objectives

After successful completion of this assignment, the student will be able to:

  • Import an Existing Video Clip
  • Edit the Video Clip
  • Export the Video Clip for use on the web

Assignment Specifications

In this assignment, you will create a short 15-20 second video using the video editing tools in Photoshop. Use any means you have available to record a clip. The video must have a still title at the beginning, beyond that everything is up to you.

Use the NTSC DV – 720 x 480 Film & Video preset, and export the finished video as an MPEG-4 (mp4).

Save your files with appropriate names, following the file naming convention: 


  yourname-video.mp4

Once completed, upload your files to Video Dropbox.

Please make sure to upload your files as PSDs. If your file is to large, please upload your PSD to the LCC Google Drive and share a link to the file with me. Then upload a JPG to the D2L Dropbox for this assignment.

If you are having problems please view the Upload guide or email me.
