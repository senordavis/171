Project Description

The homepage for LCC needs a facelift, and you have been chosen to do the layout.

The interface should consider what the primary thing you want someone to do once they have visited your site. So what is the call to action? How should a user feel when they visit your site, and what should be their lasting experience? Decide how the color scheme, typography and layout help to reinforce the message. A good place to start is by looking at the other websites, either of other colleges/universities, or just other sites in general.

Project Specifications

To satisfy the project requirements you will need to submit two versions of the homepage. The first will be for desktops at 1200 by 2000 pixels. The second will be a mobile version of the interface at 480 by 1200 pixels. 

Save your image with appropriate names, following the file naming convention:


  yourname-desktop.psd
  yourname-mobile.psd

Once completed, upload your files to Web Design Dropbox.

Please make sure to upload your files as PSDs. If your file is to large, please upload your PSD to the LCC Google Drive and share a link to the file with me. Then upload a JPG to the D2L Dropbox for this assignment.

If you are having problems please view the Upload guide or email me.
