Hi everyone, My name is Mike Davis and I am your instructor for ARTS 171, Computer Graphics / Digital Imaging.

ARTS 171 is a hands-on course utilizing Apple Macintosh computers to provide the student with an introductory understanding of Adobe Photoshop CC image manipulation software. The course will expose students to computer manipulation of a variety of images, scanning, resolution, and color issues. Basic design skills and foundational color knowledge are necessary for enrollment.

To get started please go to the Content section of the course and go through the Getting Started module. Read the Syllabus, then take the Course Syllabus Quiz.

You will find all the assignments and projects are located in a module on its own. This should help you keep track of each assignment that we work on. They will be made available at the start of every week throughout the semester. And each assignment is due at the end of each week.

Your first assignment is Photoshop Intro. You will have access to is on Thursday, August 24, 2017. For now please just read the Syllabus.
